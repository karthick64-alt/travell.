Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book a travel package?

You can book directly through our website by selecting your desired package, choosing dates, and completing the payment. Our team will confirm your booking within 24 hours.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 30 days before travel receive a full refund. Cancellations 15-30 days before receive 50% refund. Cancellations less than 15 days are non-refundable.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive travel insurance options. You can add it during the booking process or contact us for more information.

Can I customize my travel package?

Absolutely! We offer customizable packages. Contact our travel consultants to create a personalized itinerary that fits your preferences and budget.
